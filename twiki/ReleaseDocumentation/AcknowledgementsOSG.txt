---++ Software
The software and services of this OSG release come from a variety of sources as indicated in VDT release page,  %VDT_DOCS_URL%.

---++ Testing efforts
The installation, testing, and integration efforts for this release have come from a large number of people from various groups within the OSG community:
   * The OSG Trash/Integration testbed core team, and the contributed testbed sites comprising the ITB (including both CE and SE testbeds), see the SiteValidationTable.
   * The VO application developers and testers, also listed in the SiteValidationTable, for providing realistic, end-to-end testing of VO application frameworks, services and user-jobs against the deployed testbed.
   * The OSG VO coordination team  for managing liaison and coordination processes between the OSG user community and the integration team.

---++ Operational support
A thanks to the persistent ITB sites, the GOC and Fermilab for providing integration instances for central services required for full grid functional testing and validation.

---++ Documentation efforts
A *hearty thanks* to the at-large editors and reviewers (ReviewStatus) for their contributions providing content, checking, correcting, and synthesizing the topics in this ReleaseDocumentation.WebHome.
