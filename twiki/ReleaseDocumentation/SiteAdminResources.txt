---+ OSG Contacts

---++ Grid Operations Center

The Grid Operations Center is available to coordinate users, site admins and developers around an issue and additionally provide basic monitoring and troubleshooting.

---+++ Trouble Tickets 
   * Trouble tickets can be opened with the Grid Operations Center by mailing to goc@opensciencegrid.org or visiting this [[https://ticket.grid.iu.edu/goc/open][GOCTicket link]]. In order to use the Web interface you need to be an OSG registered user (registered in OIM). If you are not you can open a ticket and follow up only using the email. 
   * goc@opensciencegrid will also accept general inquiries not intended for tickets.
   * The [[https://ticket.grid.iu.edu/goc/navigator][trouble ticket system]] is also searchable, and a search may yield steps others have taken in the past to resolve related problems.

---+++ Emergency Assistance 
   * For emergencies the OSG Grid Operation Center provides extended support. Operators are on hand 24x7 at the GOC and can be reached via:
      * %ICON{person}% Phone +1 317-278-9699 
      * %ICON{mail}% Email goc@opensciencegrid.org 
      * Non-emergency issues can be opened 24x7 but will be handled during normal business hours. 

---+++ Blog 
   * The [[http://osggoc.blogspot.com/][operations blog]] contains information about recent software releases, and important outage and maintenance notifications of central OSG services.

---+++ Security 
   * For security problems you can [[https://ticket.grid.iu.edu/goc/open][submit a ticket]] or send an email to security@opensciencegrid.org. See also [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Security/WebHome][Security homepage]] or the [[http://osgsec.blogspot.com/][Security blog]].

---++ OSG Sites Coordination 

The [[Trash/SiteCoordination.WebHome][Sites Coordination]] group coordinates and facilitates the deployment OSG software to the sites and provides support and training to the OSG site administrators community. Site coordination meets (via telecon) on a monthly basis, Thursdays, usually on the second Thursday of the month, at 11am Central.  Announcements will be made to osg-sites@opensciencegrid.org, site administrators should subscribe to this list.  There are additionally training events or face to face meetings of site administrators, roughly quarterly.

---+++ Chat support 
The Site Coordination group provides regular office hours support available weekly on a regular schedule using a Campfire Web chat. The web chat is mainly targeting Site support but any OSG user is welcome to connect to receive support.
   * [[/Trash/Trash/SiteCoordination/ChatCalendar][Here]] you can find the current schedule
   * [[http://integrationcloud.campfirenow.com/6e62e][Click here]] to connect to the chat as a guest
   * You can request also login access to be able to see transcripts of previous sessions

---++ Email Lists for Support 
All the administrators of OSG or ITB resources must [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/Consortium_Mailing_Lists][subscribe to osg-sites@opensciencegrid.org]] to receive important communications. It is also recommended to subscribe to osg-int@opensciencegrid.org and other lists learn tips and tricks and to exchange knowledge with fellow OSG members. Common use-cases:
   * Send email to osg-sites@opensciencegrid.org for basic installation and configuration questions.
   * For storage services support, email osg-storage@opensciencegrid.org.
   * osg-int@opensciencegrid.org is a community list used by OSG developers and integrators to help each others.
